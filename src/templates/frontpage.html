Game Rules

You must guess the riddled four-digit number. No zero used and no number used twice. After you send your try, you'll get an answer with two digits. First - quantity of numbers, that are in riddled number. Second means, how many of them are on a proper place.

Example:
Riddled number is 8231. Your try is 1234. So you will get answer:
player try bulls cows
1234 3 2
Now you will know that overall you guessed 3 (bulls) numbers (123-4) and 2 (cows) of them are on proper place (1-23-4)
